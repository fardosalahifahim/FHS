Bangladesh National Curriculum (Classes 6 to 10)

Faridpur High School follows the Bangladesh National Curriculum as prescribed by the National Curriculum and Textbook Board (NCTB). Our curriculum is designed to foster competency-based learning, creativity, and practical skills.

Classes 6 to 8 (Junior Secondary)

All students study the following core subjects:

  • Bangla
  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science (General Science including Biology, Physics, and Chemistry basics)
  • Bangladesh and Global Studies (History, Civics, Geography)
  • Information and Communication Technology (ICT)
  • Religion Studies (Islam, Hinduism, Christianity, Buddhism)
  • Arts and Crafts
  • Physical Education and Health
  • Work and Life-Oriented Education (basic skills, vocational ideas)

Classes 9 and 10 (Secondary Level / SSC Level)

Students choose one of three groups: Science, Business Studies (Commerce), or Humanities (Arts). Each group has compulsory, elective, and optional subjects.

Science Group (Science)

Compulsory Subjects:

  • Bangla
  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Information and Communication Technology (ICT)
  • Religion
  • Bangladesh and Global Studies (BGS)

Group Electives:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Higher Mathematics (Optional subject for extra GPA advantage)

Business Studies Group (Commerce)

Compulsory Subjects:

  • Bangla
  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Information and Communication Technology (ICT)
  • Religion
  • Bangladesh and Global Studies (BGS)

Group Electives:

  • Accounting
  • Business Entrepreneurship
  • Finance and Banking

Optional Subjects: Higher Math / Agriculture Studies / another approved subject

Humanities Group (Arts)

Compulsory Subjects:

  • Bangla
  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Information and Communication Technology (ICT)
  • Religion
  • Bangladesh and Global Studies (BGS)

Group Electives:

  • History / Civics
  • Geography / Economics
  • Agriculture Studies / Home Science / Psychology

Optional Subjects: Arabic / Higher Math / another subject

SSC Exam GPA System (Class 10 Final Exam)

Students appear for the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) exam after class 10. GPA is calculated from the top 5 subjects plus optional subject bonus. The optional subject (4th subject) can increase GPA if scored high.
