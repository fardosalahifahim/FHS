Admission Information

Faridpur High School welcomes applications for new students. Please review the admission criteria, required documents, and application deadlines below.

  • Admission Criteria: Academic records, entrance exam, and interview.
  • Required Documents: Birth certificate, previous school records, passport-sized photos.
  • Application Deadline: August 31st annually.

For more details, please contact our admissions office or visit us in person.
